Entrepreneur Cell

What is E-Cell?

The Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT-Roorkee is a student-run organisation of spirited individuals who are striving to create, foster and promote entrepreneurship among the students of IIT Roorkee. The E-Cell pilots the budding enbg-initiativestrepreneurs towards their goals through all the necessary resources like mentoring, consultancy and networking with Alumni, Entrepreneurs, Investors.

Initiatives
An internship is a integral part of college life.SIP is an initiative taken to provide internship for eligible college students. SIP was formulated with the motto that none of the students should miss a chance to do an internship because of lack of opportunities. With this vision in mind, the Internship Program aims at connecting startup enthusiast graduates/undergraduates to the startups so that they get the relevant experience in their desired field.
Endeavour Entrepreneurship is a week long entrepreneurship conclave organised exclusively for the freshers batch in the autumn semester at IIT Roorkee. The basic aim is to induce an entrepreneurial mindset into the students and to air the innovative streak in them. In the 5-day extravagance we organise various workshops and informative sessions to acquaint the freshmen with the knowledge and experience of starting up from scratch. The mentors guide the teams to dig their way into the finals. On the final day, a closed-door pitching competition decides the winner of the competition.
TEDx IIT Roorkee is a full-day event organized by Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee (IITR) under the aegis of the widely popular TED (Technology, Entertainment and Design) Conferences. Our goal is to bring together luminaries to deliver idea-focused talks on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning and inspiration, and provoke conversations that matter among the bright young minds in the audience. E-cell has been the proud host of TEDx for the past 8 years.
E-Summit is the flagship event of E-Cell IIT Roorkee. This entrepreneurial festival aims to foster the zeal of entrepreneurship not only among the students of IIT Roorkee but also the participants from other colleges. E-Summit includes Startup Internships/Expo, VC lounge, Business Conclave, Panel Discussion, Networking Meets, Mentoring Sessions, Internship Fair, Competitions, Workshops and many more events aimed at developing an entrepreneurial aptitude among the participants. E-Summit witnesses the presence of eminent entrepreneurs, budding professionals, mentors, angel investors, venture capitalists, and students from different domains across the globe.
